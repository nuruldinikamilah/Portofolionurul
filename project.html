Pizza Sales Dashboard

A data analytics capstone project from the Studi Independen Bersertifikat (SIB) program, focused on the Food & Beverage (F&B) industry. The project analyzed the sales performance of a pizza restaurant that had been operating for a year in 2015. The business experienced monthly sales fluctuations, and based on the insights, the goal was to increase the average sales growth to 8% in 2016 through data analysis using SQL, data visualization in Looker Studio, and a supporting website for showcasing insights.
